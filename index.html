Welcome

Welcome to Thornhill High School, where excellence meets opportunity. Our institution has been a cornerstone of academic achievement and character development in Gweru for generations. We believe in nurturing not just the mind, but the whole person - preparing students to become confident, compassionate leaders of tomorrow.

At Thornhill, we combine rigorous academic standards with a supportive community environment. Our dedicated teachers, state-of-the-art facilities, and comprehensive programs ensure that every student has the tools they need to succeed both in the classroom and beyond.

— Thornhill High School Administration

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to achieve their full potential, develop strong character, and become responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Zimbabwe, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and the development of well-rounded individuals who excel in all aspects of life.
